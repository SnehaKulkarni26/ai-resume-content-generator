# AI Resume & Content Generator

## Goal
Create beginner-level resume and professional content using AI prompts.

## Prompt
"Write a simple and honest resume summary for a Prompt Engineering intern."

## Output
Resume Summary:
I am a motivated beginner interested in Prompt Engineering and working with AI tools. I am learning how to create clear and effective prompts to get accurate responses from language models. I have basic knowledge of AI concepts and strong communication skills. I am eager to learn, practice, and grow through real-world projects and an internship opportunity where I can improve my prompt design and problem-solving skills.

## Observation
Simple prompts produced realistic resume content.
Beginner tone helped avoid exaggeration.
